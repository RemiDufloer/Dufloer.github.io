About the Survey

We are researchers at the University of Lille, France, studying how developers handle a specific bug scenario.

This survey aims to:

  • Assess debugging strategies in the developer community.
  • Identify resolution strategies and challenges.
  • Understand developers' needs regarding debugging tools and resources.

Your participation is valuable, and we appreciate your time.

Data Handling Policy

  • Your identity remains anonymous; IP addresses are not saved.
  • No sensitive information is collected; please avoid sharing personal data.
  • You can withdraw at any time by closing the questionnaire; incomplete data will be removed.
  • The survey is hosted on LimeSurvey, and data is stored securely for research purposes only.
  • Responsible researcher: Steven Costiou (steven.costiou@inria.fr), Univ. Lille, Inria, CNRS, Centrale Lille, UMR 9189 CRIStAL, F-59000 Lille, France.
